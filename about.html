About Our Hotel

On a beachfront peninsula on the iconic Palm Jumeirah, this opulent luxury resort on the Gulf is set in gardens and comprises a manor, mansions and villas. Plush rooms and suites offer free Wi-Fi, entertainment systems and living areas, plus grand bathrooms with free-standing tubs. All have verandas with garden or gulf views. Bi-level, 2-bedroom villas on the beach feature bars and private pools. Breakfast is available. Dining options include a swanky overwater lounge with city views, and 2 restaurants by Yannick Alléno. There's a spa, a gym, a private beach, a marina and a kids' club, as well as 2 pools and tennis.

Set on an island in a striking sail-shaped building, this luxury hotel is a 5-minute walk from Wild Wadi Water Park and 4 km from Mall of the Emirates. Opulent suites feature floor-to-ceiling windows with gulf or city views. They offer Egyptian linens, designer toiletries, whirlpool baths and butler service. Upgraded suites include snooker tables, libraries, cinemas and private elevators. Parking and water park passes are free.
